Agrawal was born in Ajmer, Rajasthan , India.[2][3] His father was a senior official in the Indian
Department of Atomic Energy and his mother is a retired Economics professor from Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute in Mumbai.[4]
In 2001, he completed secondary and senior secondary studies at Atomic Energy Central School and Junior College No.4.[5]
In 2005, Agrawal obtained his Bachelor of Technology degree in computer science and engineering from IIT Bombay.[6] That year, he moved to the United States to pursue a PhD in computer science from Stanford University under the guidance of Jennifer Widom.[7][8][9] His Stanford doctoral thesis, published in 2012, is titled "Incorporating Uncertainty in Data Management and Integration".[10][11]
